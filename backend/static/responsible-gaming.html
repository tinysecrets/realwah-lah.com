🛡️ Responsible Gaming

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Gambling should be fun and entertaining, never a way to make money or solve financial problems.

Play Responsibly

  • Set a budget and stick to it
  • Never chase losses
  • Take regular breaks
  • Don't gamble when emotional or under the influence
  • Keep gambling as entertainment, not income

Warning Signs of Problem Gambling

  • Spending more money or time than intended
  • Lying about gambling activities
  • Gambling to escape problems
  • Borrowing money to gamble
  • Neglecting work, family, or responsibilities
  • Feeling guilty or anxious about gambling

Self-Help Tools

We provide the following tools to help you stay in control:

  • Deposit Limits: Contact support to set daily/weekly limits
  • Self-Exclusion: Temporarily or permanently close your account
  • Session Time Reminders: We'll remind you how long you've been playing
  • Take a Break: Cool-off period of 24 hours, 7 days, or 30 days
